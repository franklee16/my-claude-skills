# Financial Data Sources

Sources for gathering data to build HBS cases.

## Market Data

### Stock Prices & Volumes
- **Yahoo Finance** (finance.yahoo.com) — Free, historical prices, dividends, splits
- **Google Finance** — Basic price data, news
- **FRED** (fred.stlouisfed.org) — Economic data, market indices, rates
- **TradingView** — Charts, technical indicators

### Bonds & Rates
- **FRED** — Treasury yields, corporate bond spreads, Fed funds rate
- **Treasury.gov** — Official Treasury data
- **Bloomberg** — Comprehensive fixed income data (subscription)

### Commodities
- **FRED** — Commodity price indices
- **World Bank Commodity Markets** — Historical commodity prices
- **CME Group** — Futures prices, volume

### Currencies
- **FRED** — Exchange rates, trade-weighted indices
- **OANDA** — Historical FX rates

## Company Data

### SEC Filings
- **EDGAR** (sec.gov/edgar) — All public company filings
  - 10-K (annual reports)
  - 10-Q (quarterly reports)
  - 8-K (current events)
  - DEF 14A (proxy statements)
  - Form 4 (insider trading)

### Financial Statements
- **SEC EDGAR** — Official source
- **Yahoo Finance** — Summarized financials
- **Morningstar** — Financial ratios, valuation metrics
- **Seeking Alpha** — Analysis, transcripts

### Earnings Calls
- **Seeking Alpha** — Transcripts
- **Motley Fool** — Transcripts
- **Company IR pages** — Audio and transcripts

## Data Collection Workflow

1. **Start with news** — Understand the narrative
   - Search WSJ, Bloomberg, FT for event coverage
   - Identify key dates, players, decisions

2. **Gather market data** — Build exhibits
   - Yahoo Finance for price/volume data
   - FRED for economic context
   - SEC EDGAR for company-specific data

3. **Deepen with filings** — Get the details
   - 10-K for business description, risk factors
   - Earnings calls for management commentary
   - 8-K for material events

4. **Add academic context** — Ground in research
   - SSRN/NBER for relevant papers
   - Cite when discussing theory or evidence

5. **Document everything** — Source all exhibits
   - Date of access
   - URL or publication reference
   - Specific data fields used
